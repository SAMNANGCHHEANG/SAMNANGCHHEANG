External Sector
The External Sector Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Cambodia's interactions with the global economy. It presents key developments in the Balance of Payments, international trade, foreign direct investment, international reserves, and exchange rate movements to assess external stability, competitiveness, and resilience against global economic shocks.

Monetary Sector
The Monetary and Financial Dashboard provides a concise overview of Cambodia's monetary and financial developments through key indicators on money supply, credit, interest rates, exchange rates, and financial soundness. It supports the monitoring of financial conditions and banking sector performance while facilitating timely, evidence-based economic analysis and policy assessment.
